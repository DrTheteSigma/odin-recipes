Kabsa

Kabsa is an Arab mixed rice dish, served on a communal platter, that originates from Saudi Arabia And it’s commonly regarded as a national dish in all the countries of the Arabian Peninsula. It can also be found served in countries such as South of Iran, the Negev desert in Israel, and the Malabar Coast of India.

The Igredients:

  • 8 cups water
  • 4 cups basmati rice or 800 g, washed and drained
  • ½ teaspoon ground coriander

The Intructions

  1. Mix the spices
  2. Pour water
  3. Put chicken
  4. Oven for 15 min
